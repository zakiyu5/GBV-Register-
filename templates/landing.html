About Kayunga Regional Referral Hospital

Providing specialized healthcare services with compassion and excellence since 1973

Background

Kayunga RRH was established as a district Hospital in 1973 and upgraded to Regional Referral Hospital in October 2020. Located in Kayunga town, approximately 70 kilometers from Kampala, serving a catchment population of 2.5 million people.

Our Vision

"A center of excellence in the delivery of specialized health services within the region." We strive to be the leading healthcare provider through innovation, compassion, and excellence.

Our Mission

"To provide quality health services through specialized promotive, preventive, curative, training and research services to the population in the region." We are committed to holistic healthcare delivery.

Our Mandate

"To provide specialized health care, train health workers and conduct research in line with the requirements of the Ministry of Health." We serve as a training and research hub for healthcare professionals.

Our Goal

To contribute to human capital development through reduction of poverty, improve livelihood and reduction of morbidity and mortality in the region and beyond. We believe in healthcare as a foundation for development.

Our Core Values

The guiding principles that define our approach to healthcare delivery

Equity

We value and fairly treat all customers irrespective of gender, race, religion, health status, ability, ethnic background and political affiliation.

Integrity

We adhere to strong moral principles and being honest in all our dealings, maintaining the highest ethical standards in healthcare delivery.

Client Centeredness

We focus on needs that reflect priorities of service recipients, ensuring patient-centered care that respects individual dignity and preferences.

Professionalism

We adhere to professional codes of conduct and ethics, exhibiting high competence and best practices as prescribed in healthcare professions.

Teamwork

We collaborate with others to achieve common goals, recognizing that quality healthcare requires coordinated effort across disciplines.

Accountability

We are responsible and answerable for our actions, ensuring transparency and taking ownership of healthcare outcomes.

GBV Management System

A comprehensive digital solution for managing Gender-Based Violence cases with confidentiality and efficiency

Patient Registration

Secure and confidential registration of GBV survivors with comprehensive demographic and clinical information.

Follow-up Management

Automated tracking of follow-up appointments at 2 weeks, 1 month, 3 months, and 6 months intervals.

Medical Records

Complete documentation of medical interventions, test results, medications, and treatment plans.

Reporting & Analytics

Generate comprehensive reports for monitoring, evaluation, and evidence-based decision making.

Data Security

Enterprise-grade security with role-based access control, audit trails, and data encryption.
